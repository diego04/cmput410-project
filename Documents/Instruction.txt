User administration:
	 When a new user registers for the service, they are unable to log in until
	 they have been approved by an administrator. New users can be approved by
	 going to the administration screen, selecting authors, selecting the user
	 in question, and checking the approved box

Adding Friends:
    To be friends with someone, there needs to be a mutual follow.

Follow:
    To follow someone, search for authors in the navigation bar and click follow.
